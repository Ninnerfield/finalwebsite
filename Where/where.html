Where is it played?

Over the years, the World Baseball Classic has been played in various locations around the world, including the United States, Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, Puerto Rico, the Dominican Republic, Mexico, Canada, and the Netherlands. The location of the tournament is typically determined by the World Baseball Softball Confederation (WBSC), in collaboration with the various baseball federations and organizations that are involved in the tournament. The finals have taken place in the United States in all the previous tournaments.
