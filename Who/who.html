Who Plays in the WBC?

The tournament is open to players who are eligible to represent a country in international competition, which includes citizens of that country, as well as players who are eligible for citizenship through a parent or grandparent. However, players who are on an MLB 40-man roster at the time of the tournament are not eligible to participate, unless their team gives permission. Some players may not choose to participate because the tournament goes on at the same time as spring training (pre season), so players may want to work back into baseball or just focus on their major league teams. Another reason could be the team doesn't want to risk an injury so they don't give permission for the player to play.
