What is the World Baseball Classic?

The World Baseball Classic, or WBC, is the top men's baseball international tournament. It lasts only 3 weeks and players from all over the world compete in the tournament for an opportunity to represent their country. It is run by Major League Baseball (MLB) and its Player Association, under the auspices of the sport's official global governing body for international competition, the World Baseball Softball Confederation (WBSC).
