What Happens to the Winners of the WBC?

When a team wins the World Baseball Classic, they are awarded the championship trophy and earn the title of World Baseball Classic Champions. In addition to the prestige of winning the tournament, there is also a cash prize awarded to the winning team. The amount of the prize money varies depending on the edition of the tournament, but it can be several million dollars. The prize money is typically split among the members of the winning team and coaching staff. Winning the event can make players who were not well known, very famous and popular.
